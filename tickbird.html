Why tickbird sits on hippo's bird

There was once a time when hippo and tickbird were not friends at all. Tickbird was fed up to the beak with hippo's splishing and sploshing. Then one day tickbird challenged hippo to a contest as to who could drink the most water in a single gulp. Hippo could not believe her ears. Big hippo versus tiny tickbird. But little tickbird outwit hippo by cleverly using the tides and the sea coming in and going out. From that day on hippo is not allowed to splosh in the waterhole without permission and tickbird is allowed to sit on hippo's back and eat all the yummy ticks she can eat!
