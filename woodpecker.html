Why woodpecker pecks

There was a time when woodpecker did not peck. All the birds had their very own sound, but woodpecker did not make any sound at all. Then one very dark night in the jungle, the moon and the stars disappeared. The animals summoned the birds to tell lion but woodpecker could not pass the message on. Distraught, woodpecker flew up to the skies but could not find trace of the stars nor the moon. In desperation, woodpecker pecked at the dark sky and a star came back, then another, then another until the sky was now full of bright stars. Everyone was delighted, no beast more than woodpecker who now had her very own special sound: pecking.
